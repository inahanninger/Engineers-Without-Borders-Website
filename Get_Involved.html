How To Get Involved In EWBOx

There are three main ways to become involved in Engineers Without Borders Oxford:

Join a project
- You can join one of the many projects already running as part of EWBOx, further details about these projects and who to contact regarding joining can be foundhere.
Start a project
-If you have an idea for a project that you believe fits within Engineers Without Borders then we can help provide resources such as keen student helpers, money and equipment. We are always excited for new projects and if you would like to discuss in more detail please contact the president at president@ewbox.org
Join the Committee
-Elections for Engineers Without Borders Oxford’s executive committee take place each year towards the start of Trinity Term. If you are interested in running for the executive committee (positions found here), don’t hesitate to contact the current committee members to find out more about what is involved.
-Alternatively look at the current vacancies below and if you are interested email in with what you can bring to the role.
